Donald Trump was borned on the 14th of June, 1946. In the borough of Queens. which is a part of New York City. His full name is Donald John Trump. And he actually grow up in a rather large family. His father Fred Trump, was a highly successful businessman. Who made a living building houses in New York. His mother Marian Macleod Trump, was originally an immigrant from Scotland. Donald was the fourth of five children in the family. His siblings are Marian, Fred Junior, Elisabeth and Robert. 

Although Donald growed up in a wealthy household, his parents always told him the importance of hard work from very young age. They wanted to make sure he understood the value of money and effort. 

As a young boy, Donald attended a private school called the Kew-Forest school. Later on, his father decided to send him to a military boarding school, known as the New York Military Academy. His father did this for a reason, he thought the Military school will teach Donald to be disciplined. This would help Donald to be a stronger person.

After finishing high school, Donald continued his education by studying at Fordham University in New York for two years.
Following that, he transffered to the Wharton school at the University of Pennsylvania. Which is a very presitigious annd famous business school.

He eventually graduated at 1968, with degree in economics, ready to take on the business world. After finishing his university studies, Donald began working directly for his father's company, the Trump orginization. He was given a lot of responsibility early on. He was put in charge of running the day to day business, and developing brand new projects. Because his father had already made significant amount of money building houses. Donald was able to use those finacial resources to expand the company even further.

In the 1970s, he took on his very first major project, he helped to redevelope the Commodore Hotel in New York City. At that time the hotel was struggling and not making much money. But Donald working incredibly hard to transform it into a famous luxury destination. He later renamed it the Grand Hyatt.
