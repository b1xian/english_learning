It means "wood" or "tree" in English.
Let's learn it through a pictographic method, I'm sure you'll remember it in 3 minutes and never forget it.

🔍
First, let's take a look at how this character evolved:
[check the first img below]
In the beginning, it looked like a tree. 🌳
Over time, it grandually evolved into the form we use today "木" (mù).

📌
This character was widely used to form many Chinese characters and words.
Let me give you a few common examples to help you understand (you don't need to memorize them)

🌲 林 (lín)
It’s made up of two "木" characters.
It represents many trees standing together, so it means "woods" or "grove".

🌲🌲 森 (sēn)
It’s made up of three "木" characters.
It represents even more trees, so it also relates to a forest.
The word “森林” means “forest” in English. 🌳

🌿 树 (shù)
The left part is "木", this character means "tree".
And many characters with "木" on the left are related to trees or wood.
